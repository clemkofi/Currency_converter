About this App

This app was made to help users to easily convert their currencies to other currencies. Individuals as we well as business owners can easily convert any currency of their choice to another. This makes conducting business a breeze and takes out the stress of always having to look online for conversion rates. Other people people prefer to pull out their calculators for such takes. To this we say no more!

We refresh our exchange rates on an hourly basis so you can be rest-assured that you are getting the most current exchange rates. We search the most prominent exchange rates providers and show the most consistent rate for that particular hour. We assure of the best rates all the time.
